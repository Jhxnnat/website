In December of 2025, some friends of mine, *Mono* and *Luencaro*, came to visit to the town. As always, we started the classic ephemeral Minecarft server that has a expiration date before it's been created. Normally the server would just last as long as the visit duration, but this time we were discussing about actually making this our forever world. We played for hours almost every day, we built farm, bases, I even had a war with one of my friends who's the one who knows most redstone.

{{ resize_image(alt_text="Luencaro and Jhona before the war" path="/minecraft/luencaro-jhona-0.png", width=622, height=415, op="fit_width") }}

Basically what happened is that *Mono* robbed me and killed me. I got angry so I exiled myself and declared war to him, wandered for thousand and thousand of blocks to finally came back prepared for vengeance. 

I allied with my other friend and started to grind a lot to get op. We did several secret operations, and even had a secret base. This was us going into a bastion:

{{ resize_image(alt_text="Luencaro and Jhona preparing for the war in the nether" path="/minecraft/luencaro-jhona-nether.png", width=622, height=415, op="fit_width") }}

After and incredibly prepared mission with the method *just rush and go all in*, and also avoiding some underground explosives under out base, *Luencaro* and me killed *Mono*, and made a peace treatment. 
 
After those events the server really peaceful, I even created a little scavenger hunt for my friends where the bounty were cool op items.

{{ resize_image(alt_text="Peace book" path="/minecraft/peace-book.png", width=622, height=415, op="fit_width") }}

But the vacations came to an end, and even tho I bough a hosting for the world for 1 month they didn't play a lot because of college.

{{ resize_image(alt_text="Mono, Luencaro and Me chilling on Mono's house" path="/minecraft/mono-luencaro-jhona-0.png", width=622, height=415, op="fit_width") }}

Eventually I decided to take the word as my personal *forever world*, and I've been playing on it for about more than a month, my sister recently started playing with me too.

And so right know my plans are to build a lot of tiny and big builds. My idea is that the build should be useful in addition to being pretty, I won't be building to imitate real life buildings but to try to explore different styles depending on the context of the build.
